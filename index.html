Built to Move Lagos Forward

Bica Driver was founded to solve a real challenge — finding reliable, trustworthy drivers in Lagos — while creating meaningful opportunities for skilled driving professionals across the city.

Under the leadership of Adeniyi Christopher Awotundun, Bica Driver has become a trusted bridge between car owners who need dependable expertise and professional drivers who deserve quality opportunities.
